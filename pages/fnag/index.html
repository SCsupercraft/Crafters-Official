Five Nights At Gru's


FNAG is a horror game based on FNAF. There are 9 rooms: your office, the left corridoor, the right corridoor, the stage, Macho Cove, the toilets, the storage room, the lab and the party room. Your goal is to survive until 6am while the Despicable Me characters try to kill you.
